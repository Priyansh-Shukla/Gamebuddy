The Abandoned Dungeon is a side area accessed by dropping through a well in a side courtyard of Ashina Castle. It is the game's tonal hard-left: damp stone corridors, caged human experiment subjects, oversized rats, undead patients in white robes, and a stretch of underground burial sites lit by guttering torches. The atmosphere is closer to a survival-horror corridor than to the rooftop swordplay of the castle above it.

Mechanically, the dungeon is a connective tissue area more than a destination in itself. It branches into two important locations: the path to Ashina Depths (one of the mid-game regions) and a stairwell to a small ritual chamber where the Senpou Temple monks have been conducting medical experiments. A Shichimen Warrior — one of the apparition-tier mini-bosses — is encountered partway through, in a small flooded chamber.

The dungeon also contains the NPC Doujun, a Senpou monk performing illicit experiments, whose questline yields the Red and White Pinwheel and provides one of the game's darker side stories. Several Prayer Beads and Gourd Seeds are tucked into the area's corners, and the path through it is short enough that most players visit it once and return only when collecting late-game checklist items.

Trivia: the cages and the language of "patients" in the Abandoned Dungeon are deliberate hints at the in-universe origin of the Mibu rejuvenating waters and the Senpou Temple's experiments on immortality, content the player will see more directly later.
