Hirata Estate Revisited is the second memory pass through the Hirata Estate area, unlocked by offering the Father's Bell Charm at the Dilapidated Temple Buddha after the Purification-ending dialogue chain. The geography is recognizable — the same burning forest, courtyard, lord's residence, and hidden temple — but the enemy layout has been replaced by elite shinobi patrols and several new mini-bosses, and the lighting and music are subtly retuned.

The path through the revisited memory is short and direct. Two new mini-boss encounters (Shigekichi of the Red Guard and a Lone Shadow Vilehand in the courtyard) gate the player toward the inner audience hall, and a small number of new pickups appear along the way. The route culminates in the hidden temple beneath the lord's residence — the same arena where Lady Butterfly was originally fought — for the rematch with the Great Shinobi Owl in his prime, before the events of his "presumed death."

Defeating Owl (Father) in the revisited memory yields the Aromatic Flower — the Everblossom — which is the key item required to trigger the Purification ending at the final-boss sequence. The area is mandatory only for the Purification branch; players pursuing the Immortal Severance or Return endings can skip it entirely.

Trivia: the revisit is structurally one of the cleanest examples of FromSoftware's "echo level" design philosophy, in which an early-game area is re-presented under late-game conditions to mirror the player's increased capability against the same physical space.
