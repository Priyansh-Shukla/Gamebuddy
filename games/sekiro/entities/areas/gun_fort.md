The Gun Fort is a small fortified cliffside complex deep within Sunken Valley, defended by riflemen and reached through a chain of grapple points and rope bridges. It is technically a sub-area of the valley rather than a region of its own, but it is structurally important enough to warrant its own progression node: it gates the path to the Guardian Ape's arena.

Entry to the Gun Fort requires the Gun Fort Shrine Key, which is held by Kuro and only handed over after the player has completed enough of Senpou Temple to satisfy the related dialogue trigger. Players who attempt to brute-force into the fort without the key find the gate locked. This is the single most common progression confusion in the mid-game — players assume the fort is a Sunken Valley problem when it is actually gated by Senpou progression.

Inside the fort, the path is short: a stretch of ranged-combat corridors, a final cliffside drop, and a transition into the Guardian Ape's arena pit. The fort also contains the Gourd Seed merchant in the Sunken Valley region and several Spirit Emblem pickups, but its primary function is as connective tissue to the next major boss.

Trivia: the locked-gate-needs-Kuro mechanic is one of several places where the game uses player-to-Kuro dialogue as an implicit checkpoint, reinforcing that Wolf's quest is contingent on his lord's instructions rather than his own initiative.
