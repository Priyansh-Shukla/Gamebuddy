Owl (Father) is the late-game memory rematch with the Great Shinobi Owl, fought in the hidden temple beneath the lord's residence in Hirata Estate Revisited — the same arena where the player originally fought Lady Butterfly. He is structurally a tougher version of the Ashina Castle Owl fight, with faster timings, an expanded prosthetic-mirror toolkit, and a new opening phase built around aerial throws.

The fight has two phases. Phase one is a tightened version of the castle Owl moveset: smoke-bomb mixups, perilous-grab tells from a hunched lunge stance, and a Firecrackers-equivalent flash that he uses defensively. Phase two introduces an Owl-summoned Ashina Cross stance — a Combat Art the player can also learn — and a long-range feather-throw projectile attack that fills the arena with damage spheres for several seconds. The fight is widely considered one of the hardest non-final-boss encounters in the game and is a clear late-game skill check.

Defeating Owl (Father) yields a Memory, the Everblossom (the Aromatic Flower key item required for the Purification ending), and the Aged Feather Mist Raven prosthetic upgrade material. The fight is also the canonical character resolution for Wolf's adoptive-father relationship and is unmissable for any player pursuing the Purification branch.

Trivia: the Ashina Cross stance the Owl uses in phase two is, in-universe, the technique he taught Wolf — the same Combat Art the player can purchase in the Ashina Arts tree — and the fight is the only place in the game where it is canonically used against the player by the character who invented it.
