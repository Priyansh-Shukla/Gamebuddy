The Great Shinobi Owl is the late-game boss fought on the central rooftop of a burning Ashina Castle after the player chooses to break the Iron Code and stay loyal to Kuro. He is a tall, heavily-built shinobi in a red sash, fighting with a long curved blade, a smoke-bomb arsenal, and the same shinobi-prosthetic toolkit that Wolf himself uses — making him a structural mirror of the player character.

The fight has two phases. Phase one is a tight duel built around mixups between fast horizontal strings, a perilous-grab tell that drags Wolf forward, a Firecrackers-equivalent flash that opens punish windows for the Owl, and an Antidote-Powder-equivalent buff he applies mid-fight to neutralize one of Wolf's prosthetic options. Phase two is mechanically similar but adds an Owl's-Eye perilous-thrust that demands Mikiri at a noticeably faster cadence than the earlier game, and a small-area smoke explosion that limits visibility.

Defeating the Great Shinobi Owl is the unlock point for the rest of the late game. It opens the Inner Sanctum visit (for the Purification-ending aromatic-flower chain), the Rice-and-Frozen-Tears Divine Child interaction (for the Return ending), the Demon of Hatred encounter at Ashina Outskirts, and the final-boss sequence at Ashina Reservoir.

Trivia: the Owl's prosthetic-shinobi toolkit visually rhymes with Wolf's, and the fight is one of the most direct mechanical statements the game makes that Wolf and his adoptive father trained from the same playbook.
