Seven Ashina Spears: Shikibu Toshikatsu Yamauchi is a mini-boss encountered in the late-game state of Ashina Reservoir. He is a member of the elite Ashina Spears unit, fought in a small rooftop courtyard, wielding a long polearm and a deep stance built around lunging thrust attacks and wide horizontal sweeps. He is the second Seven Spears mini-boss the player will encounter; the first appears much earlier in a different role, and he serves as a clear progression marker for late-game posture mastery.

Mechanically the fight is a pure deflection-and-Mikiri drill. His thrust attacks are perilous and Mikiri-vulnerable; his sweep attacks are perilous and must be jumped. Almost every other attack is a standard polearm string that rewards held deflection. The Loaded Spear is the canonical prosthetic counter — its thrust outranges his guard and produces clean Posture pressure — and the fight typically falls in two or three Deathblow cycles to a player who has internalized late-game timing.

Defeating Shikibu yields a Prayer Bead. The fight is also the last canonical posture-duel warmup before the final boss sequence, and is generally considered a cleaner skill check than several earlier mini-bosses despite its mid-tier reputation.

Trivia: the Seven Ashina Spears were a real Sengoku-era elite spear unit attached to the Asakura clan; the in-game Spears are a clear visual and naming reference, and the polearm stances in their movesets are modeled on period spear-fencing manuals.
