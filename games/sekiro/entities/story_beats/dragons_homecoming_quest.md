The Dragon's Homecoming quest is the side-quest chain that unlocks the Return ending. It is structurally the most time-sensitive of the four ending branches and requires several specific NPC interactions performed in a specific order across the late game.

The chain begins by speaking to the Divine Child of Rejuvenation in the Inner Sanctum of Senpou Temple. After defeating the Great Shinobi Owl, returning to her and asking for rice on consecutive rest cycles eventually causes her to "fall asleep" — at which point the player receives the Rice for Kuro item and must deliver it to Kuro at Ashina Castle. The interaction then continues: the Divine Child wakes again and asks Wolf to retrieve the Holy Chapter: Dragon's Return, a text held by an old woman near a specific Sculptor's Idol in the Ashina Reservoir area.

The chain requires the player to also collect the Fresh and Dried Serpent Viscera — two organs taken from the Great Serpent encounters in Sunken Valley and Senpou Temple. The Divine Child consumes both viscera and the chapter to transform into a vessel for the Dragon's Heritage, and produces the Frozen Tears, the second key item alongside the Divine Dragon's Tears required for the Return ending.

At the final-boss sequence, offering both Frozen Tears and Divine Dragon's Tears triggers the Return ending cutscene — Sekiro and the Divine Child journey west to return the Dragon's Heritage to its origin, the most narratively complete of the four resolutions.
